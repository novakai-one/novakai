inside `initLayout` (e.g. `n`→`node`, `f`/`foot`→`box`, `e`→`edge`, `g`→`groupId`,

renames with call sites updated. Also, to additionally clear the two `max-params`
warnings, bundled `placeSatelliteClusters`'s `(foot, horizontal, clusterBase)` and
`placeSatellites`'s `(foot, horizontal)` params into single object arguments,
reducing `placeSatellites` from 5 params to 4 and `placeSatelliteClusters` from 6
to 4, updating the one call site in `autoLayout` accordingly.

Result before the gate check: eslint warnings on the target dropped from 92 to 7
(the residual 4 are `w`/`h` id-length hits forced by the `Footprint`/`DiagramNode`
shape, and 1 max-lines-per-function on `initLayout` itself, unfixable within the
300-line diff budget since fixing it requires de-indenting the entire ~480-line
closure to module scope). Diff was 131 additions / 126 deletions (257 changed
lines), within the 300-line budget.

Root cause: `placeSatellites` is a module-private (unexported) helper, but the
flowmap architecture map (`docs/flowmap/_bundle.mmd`) tracks it as a drilled
function-level node with a recorded arity of 5. Bundling two of its parameters
into one object argument (done to clear the `max-params` lint warning — a
category not actually named in this pass's specific orders, which called out
only "duplicate strings, local renames, functions over 60 lines") changed its
arity to 4, which the flowmap conformance gate (run inside `spec:test:all` via
`tools/flowmap/onboard.test.mjs`) flags as code/map drift, independent of the
TypeScript exported-surface check in step 6.

## What was reverted
`git reset --hard` on `m6/io-layout-r2-p1`, discarding all uncommitted edits to
`src/io/layout.ts`. Branch is back at `b2616ce` (tip of `m6/integration` at
branch-creation time, "refactor(m6): src/interaction/pointer.ts readability
pass"). No commit was made for this pass.

## Lesson for the next attempt
Do not touch `max-params` on internal functions that flowmap drills to
function-level (check `docs/flowmap/_bundle.mmd` / run `flowmap:ship` first, or
just stick to renames + line-count fixes only, since the pass order never
named max-params as an in-scope category for this pass).
